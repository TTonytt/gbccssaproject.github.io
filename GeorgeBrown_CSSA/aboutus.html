About Us

Our primary mission is to promote the awareness of the Chinese culture on campus through the establishment of student connections via community service events and social events and to unify Chinese students throughout the community.

GEORGE BROWN COLLEGE CHINESE STUDENTS' AND SCHOLARS ASSOCIATION 简称 G B C C S S A, 是由乔治布朗学院的中国学生学者所建立的非营利， 非政治，和非宗教社团组织。该组织旨在从学术，职业发展，生活，文娱等全方面服务于GBC中国学生，学者。 G B C C S S A作为中国学生学者与中国驻美大使馆沟通的桥梁，积极维护中国学生，学者在海外的合法权益。
